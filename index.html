    • Welcome to Pudukottai District Silambattam Association

    The Pudukkottai District Silambam Association is a proud custodian of Silambam, the ancient martial art of Tamil Nadu. Established with the vision to preserve, promote, and propagate this indigenous tradition, we serve as the official district body affiliated to the Tamil Nadu Silambattam Association and recognized by the Tamil Nadu government.

    Through our widespread network of Silambam training centers, qualified masters (gurus), and certified events, we aim to foster discipline, physical fitness, self-defense skills, and Tamil cultural pride among todayâ€™s youth.

    What is Silambam?

    Silambam is a traditional Indian martial art form rooted in Tamil culture, renowned for its dynamic footwork, agility, and the use of bamboo staffs and weapons. Beyond combat, Silambam embodies philosophy, ethics, and ancient Tamil values.

    Our Mission

    • To promote Silambam as a mainstream martial art and sport.

    • Train students in authentic, scientific methods.
    • To organize district-level, state-level, and national competitions.
    • To certify coaches, referees, and athletes through a structured evaluation system.
    • To bring global recognition to Silambam as a heritage sport of Tamil Nadu.
