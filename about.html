    • About Us

    The Pudukkottai District Silambattam Association is proudly affiliated with the Tamil Nadu Silambattam Association, which in turn is recognized by the Indian Silambam Federation. Our district stands as a beacon of excellence and dedication to preserving and promoting the ancient Tamil martial art of Silambam.

    Pudukkottai is one of the top two districts in Tamil Nadu in terms of state and national Silambam achievements. Our district is renowned for producing a large number of medal-winning athletes, certified referees, and highly qualified coaches. We are proud to say Pudukkottai holds the highest number of registered Silambam players in India.

    Our aim is to foster discipline, tradition, fitness, and pride among the youth while nurturing future champions through certified training, structured competitions, and mentorship from experienced masters.

    Our Mission & Vision

    Our mission is to promote Silambam as a mainstream martial art and sport, ensuring its preservation and growth through structured training, competitions, and community engagement.

    We envision a future where Silambam is recognized globally as a heritage sport of Tamil Nadu, inspiring generations to embrace its values
